# Hyperframes Primer (M0 placeholder)

> This is a short stub. The real primer is generated by
> `python scripts/snapshot_primer.py` in M1 per `.claude/plans/08-primer-snapshot.md`.

Hyperframes renders HTML to deterministic, frame-by-frame video.

## Composition rules

- Every composition has a root `<div data-composition-id="..." data-width="W" data-height="H" data-duration="D">`.
- Timed elements carry `class="clip"` and `data-start`, `data-duration`, `data-track-index`.
- `<video>`, `<audio>`, and composition divs MUST NOT have `class="clip"` — the framework manages their visibility.
- Sub-compositions live under `compositions/` wrapped in `<template id="<composition-id>-template">…</template>`.
- GSAP timelines register on `window.__timelines["<composition-id>"]` and must be `paused: true`.
- Only deterministic logic — no `Date.now()`, no `Math.random()`, no `fetch`.

## Archetypes

- NARRATIVE_SCENE — characters, emotional beats, a twist, literary phrasing.
- PRODUCT_PROMO — product name, feature list, brand words, "X seconds".
- DATA_EXPLAINER — numbers, "X went up by Y%", datasets, "explain".
- UI_WALKTHROUGH — "app", "flow", "user goes through".
- SOCIAL_CARD — "Instagram post", "tweet", "follow banner".
